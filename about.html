Elite Customs

Elite Customs specializes in customer satisfaction. That is why the base model of any cleat can be changed, and most cleats are availiable in many, if not any colors.

Colin Doles

Colin Doles is a highschool football player at James Bowie Highschool in Austin, TX who was inspired to start making custom cleats by other cleat customizers. Colin has always had a passion for football (and cleats), which led him here, Elite Customs.
